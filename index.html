About Me

Hi! I'm Federico Rossendy, a software engineering student at Universidad Nacional de La Matanza with a passion for technology, creativity, and problem-solving.

Working with Java and Python, I’ve developed projects like a password manager and Relic Market, where I applied both technical expertise and collaborative problem-solving.

Beyond coding, I’m diving into 3D modeling and Unreal Engine to create immersive gaming experiences, while also exploring data analysis and teamwork-driven solutions.

My goal? To grow as a versatile developer, building innovative software, analyzing complex data, and crafting unique games.
